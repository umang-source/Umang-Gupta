Iron Ventures Limited

Strategic capital for frontier technologies

We are not a traditional venture capital fund and we do not lead rounds. We participate selectively in co-investments, secondaries and bridge financings where there is a clear strategic fit and strong cross-border potential.

Series A/B+ Co-Investments
We invest alongside established lead VCs where a lead investor is already in place.
Secondary Liquidity
We consider secondary share transactions for founder and early employee liquidity.
Bridge Financing
We support bridge rounds between Seed, Series A and Series B where timing and conviction align.
Investment Focus

Where we invest

Typical ticket size is $2–10M, with a preference for strategic cross-border opportunities across deep technical markets.

Quantum
Quantum computing, sensing, communications and enabling technologies with long-term strategic relevance.
AI Infrastructure
Datacentres, GPUs, power, cooling, connectivity and the physical infrastructure required to scale AI — including edge inference, neuromorphic computing and on-device AI.
Deeptech Healthcare
Advanced diagnostics, medical devices, imaging, AI-enabled clinical workflows and healthcare infrastructure.
Photonics
Light-based technologies across sensing, imaging, communications, computing and industrial applications.
Energy Transition
Battery energy storage (BESS), grid-scale storage, next-generation energy systems and infrastructure enabling the transition to clean, resilient power.
Space & New Space
Satellite infrastructure, launch systems, earth observation, in-orbit services and the commercial space economy across UK, India, Middle East and Asia.
Defence & Dual-Use
Autonomous systems, advanced sensing, cybersecurity and dual-use technologies benefiting from Europe's post-2022 defence investment wave.
Advanced Materials
Semiconductors, metamaterials, next-generation composites and engineered materials that underpin quantum, photonics, energy and defence applications.
Robotics & Autonomy
Industrial automation, surgical robotics, autonomous logistics and intelligent systems bridging AI, healthcare and physical infrastructure.
